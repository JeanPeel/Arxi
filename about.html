About Arxi Paints

I'm Ari Peel, a 17 year old artist specializing in digital paintings, but I work in all media. Arxi Paints is my artist brand and tag. I just graduated from American Academy of Innovation, an amazing tech oriented high school. I like going on regular walks through the neighborhood. I just got 2 pet snails that I named Hardy and Darwin (there are snails in some of my art, but its not them). This is my art gallery, I hope that your enjoy looking at it as much as I enjoyed making it.
